Design Consultation FAQ

What's the difference between this and a free consultation?

Our design consultation is a comprehensive professional service that includes detailed recommendations, written summaries, and specific plant/design suggestions. Free consultations are for general inquiries and project discussions.

Do I need to hire you for installation after the consultation?

Absolutely not! The consultation stands alone as a complete service. You're free to implement the recommendations yourself or hire any contractor you choose.

How detailed are the recommendations?

Very detailed! You'll receive specific plant names, quantities, placement suggestions, care instructions, and implementation steps in writing.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

We stand behind our work! If you're not completely satisfied with the consultation, we'll work with you to make it right or provide a full refund.

What should I prepare before the consultation?

Think about your goals, budget range, and any specific challenges. We'll handle the technical assessment, but your vision and preferences are essential.

Do you offer follow-up consultations?

Yes! Additional consultations are available as your project evolves. We also offer phone/email support for questions after implementation.
