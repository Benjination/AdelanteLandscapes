Landscape Designer & Environmental Visionary

Our Founder

Cheryl Beesley is a landscape designer and visionary with over 30 years of experience in the field. Holding a master's degree in landscape architecture and a focus in horticulture and environmental sustainability, she is renowned for creating breathtakingly beautiful and functional outdoor spaces that seamlessly blend aesthetics with practicality.

As a native Texan, Beesley has a deep understanding of the region's unique plant palette, climate, and soil conditions, which she skillfully incorporates into a diverse range of designs spanning modern, natural, and sustainable styles.

What sets Beesley apart is her innovative approach, often incorporating elements of rainwater harvesting and edible plants into her designs. She has a talent for seamlessly integrating fruit-bearing trees, vegetables, and herbs, creating a harmonious blend of beauty and functionality that reflects her commitment to environmental stewardship and promoting a healthier lifestyle.

In addition to her acclaimed design work, Beesley is the author of the best-selling book "Landscaping with Edible Plants in Texas," inspiring countless homeowners and professionals.

Design Philosophy

Sustainability First

Every design prioritizes environmental responsibility, water conservation, and support for local ecosystems.

Functional Beauty

Landscapes should be both stunning and practical, serving the needs of families while enhancing property value.

Native Adaptation

Using Texas-native plants and understanding local climate conditions to create resilient, low-maintenance gardens.

Edible Integration

Seamlessly incorporating food-producing plants into beautiful landscape designs for healthier living.
