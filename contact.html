Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer design consultations?

Yes! We offer professional design consultations for $75. This includes an on-site visit, detailed recommendations, and a written summary. Learn more about our design consultation service.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Fort Worth and the North Texas region, including Dallas-Fort Worth Metroplex and surrounding communities.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Small gardens may take 1-2 weeks, while larger landscapes can take 4-8 weeks or more.

Do you offer maintenance services?

Yes, we provide ongoing maintenance services to keep your landscape healthy and beautiful throughout the seasons.

Can you work with existing landscapes?

Absolutely! We can enhance, renovate, or partially redesign existing landscapes to improve their beauty and functionality.

What makes your designs sustainable?

We focus on native plants, water-wise irrigation, soil health, and creating landscapes that work with Texas climate and natural systems.
