Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! I offer complimentary initial consultations for projects within my service area. This allows us to meet, discuss your vision, and determine if we're a good fit for working together.

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Simple installations might take 1-2 weeks, while complete landscape transformations can take 4-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the proposal phase.

Do you work with specific budgets?

Absolutely! I work with clients across a range of budgets and can often phase projects to spread costs over time. During our consultation, we'll discuss budget-friendly options and prioritize elements.

Do you provide maintenance services?

Yes, I offer ongoing maintenance services to keep your landscape thriving. This includes seasonal care, pruning, plant health monitoring, and system maintenance.

Can you work with existing landscapes?

Definitely! Many projects involve enhancing or renovating existing landscapes. I can work with your current plants and features while adding new elements to achieve your goals.

What makes your approach different?

My focus on sustainable, edible, and native plant landscapes sets me apart. I emphasize creating beautiful spaces that also provide food, support local ecosystems, and require minimal water and maintenance.
